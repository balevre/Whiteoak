Our Story

In 2016, an idea was hatched out of the desire to learn something new. We read over various craft books and blogs looking for something that would be enjoyable to do on a reguralr basis. We stumbled upon a great book about how to make soap, from there we began to experiment. We found that the mix of innovation and science that is required to make useful soaps was a process that was educational, and practical. From soap, we began to explore the idea of making candles. We discovered that the process to make candles is somewhat similar to making soap. We began making candles and soap on a regular basis, letting family and friends give us opinions of our products. From those simple orgins of wanting to learn something new, we have grown into a small batch chandlery. Please feel free to get in touch with ideas or comments. We love hearing from everyone.
